Our Story

Driven by Quality & Trust

Janish Enterprises started as a small shop with a simple goal — make it easy for mechanics and garage owners to find the tools and spare parts they actually need, at fair prices.

Today, we are an authorised dealer for trusted brands like Penta, Kapson, and Titan. We supply grease pumps, hydraulic jacks, hand tools, power tools, and spare parts to workshops across the region.

Every product we stock is chosen for its durability and performance. We don't just sell tools — we help you get the job done right.
